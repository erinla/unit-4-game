Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each rock crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will also change.
